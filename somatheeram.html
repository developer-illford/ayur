Somatheeram has already been awarded 10 times as “Best Ayurvedic Centre” in Kerala. About Somatheeram Ayurveda Health Resort

Here is no doubt that the traditional Ayurveda teaching is one of the most important components of Indian culture. It is the centre of Somatheeram, the first Ayurvedic retreat center to make Ayurveda known beyond the borders of India. The facility owes its more than 30 years of experience with international guests to its good relations with Europe. Somatheeram is located directly at the beach, near Kovalam at Trivandrum. The Ayurveda retreat centre is based on a holistic concept that reflects both the culture and beauty of India. The aspiration: to offer Ayurveda, Yoga and Meditation in a relaxed and fascinating environment.
