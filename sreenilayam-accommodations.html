AYURVEDAM SREENILAYAM

The Home of Genuine Ayurveda

Ayurvedam At Sreenilayam

We are a small, family-run Ayurvedic treatment center tucked away in a serene village about an hour outside of Calicut in Kerala, India. Ayurveda is our way of life, handed down from generation to generation, and we offer this authentic Ayurveda to you. Our patients consider Sreenilayam a healing haven: a place where they come not just for rejuvenation of the body, but also of mind and spirit.

We put great love and care into every detail at Sreenilayam. We have seven rooms and four on-site doctors so that we can offer the highest quality treatment to our patients. We make many of our own Ayurvedic medicines and oils, our meals are made with all local organic ingredients, our purified drinking water comes from our own well itself, we are powered by solar energy, and we built the center according to Vastu (Vedic sacred geometry) for proper flow of energy. It’s a peaceful oasis where you can reconnect with the nature around you and your own nature within.

ACCOMMODATION

Embrace the warmth of Kerala’s Ayurvedic heritage where every smile and space tells a story of healing and harmony. More than a stay it’s a journey into the timeless traditions of Ayurveda and soulful connection

Our Ayurvedic homestay features seven serene rooms, a peaceful library, an organic garden, a dedicated yoga studio, three treatment rooms, a private kitchen, a welcoming dining area, free Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, and a walkway that circles the entire property for tranquil strolls amidst nature.

Rooms

Ayurveda at Sreenilayam embodies the true essence of authentic Ayurveda. Built in traditional Nalukettu style and aligned with Vastu, it harmonizes Kerala’s heritage with modern comfort. Spacious rooms feature private bathrooms, Western toilets, and cozy beds, maintained with daily cleaning.

Inside the Sreenilayam Where Serenity Meets Ayurveda

Guests can enjoy wholesome, Sattvic cuisine in a warm and welcoming dining area, promoting digestion and vitality. Modern comforts such as free Wi-Fi and laundry facilities are provided with care, ensuring a seamless stay without disturbing the retreat’s natural rhythm.
