AYURVEDA

Abhyangam

Abhyangam is a traditional full-body Ayurvedic oil massage performed by two therapists working in synchronized movements. It uses warm, medicated herbal oils tailored to an individual’s body type (dosha).

Purpose & Benefits:

  • Improves blood circulation
  • Relieves muscle tension and joint stiffness
  • Promotes detoxification through the skin
  • Nourishes tissues and enhances skin health
  • Calms the nervous system and promotes deep relaxation
Shirodhara

About Shirodhara:

Shirodhara is a deeply relaxing Ayurvedic therapy where a continuous stream of warm, medicated oil (or other therapeutic liquids) is gently poured over the forehead, particularly on the ‘third eye’ region.

Purpose & Benefits:

  • Calms the mind and relieves stress and anxiety
  • Improves sleep quality and treats insomnia
  • Enhances mental clarity and concentration
  • Balances the nervous system
  • Promotes deep relaxation and emotional well-being
Nasyam
Ayurvedic healing experience of Nasyam

Nasyam is one of the Panchakarma therapies in Ayurveda, involving the gentle administration of medicated oils or herbal extracts through the nostrils. It is often preceded by a mild head and face massage and herbal steam therapy (as seen in the image) to open up nasal passages.

Purpose & Benefits:

  • Clears nasal congestion and sinus issues
  • Improves breathing and strengthens sensory organs
  • Relieves headaches, migraines, and stress
  • Enhances mental clarity and concentration
  • Promotes balance in the Prana Vayu (life force energy)
