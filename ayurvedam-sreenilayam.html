AYURVEDA ACHARYA

DR. MALI

Chief Ayurvedic Physician | Founder of Ayurvedam Sreenilayam

Dr. Mali is a seasoned Ayurvedic physician with over 37 years of clinical experience in the field of traditional Indian medicine. He is the founder and chief physician of Ayurvedam Sreenilayam, nestled in the serene village of Chathamangalam, along the coast of river cherupuzha Kozhikode, Kerala.Dr. Mali journey in Ayurveda began with a rigorous seven-and-a-half-year Gurukula training at Coimbatore Ayurveda College, where he immersed himself in classical Ayurvedic education under the traditional Gurukulam system. He later honed his practical skills while working with various clinics under The Arya Vaidya Pharmacy (Coimbatore) Ltd.Born into a family deeply rooted in Ayurveda and astrology, Dr. Mali carries forward the legacy of his father, Vaidyapadan Sri Dr. Ramakrishnan, a renowned graduate of Shoranur Ayurveda College.Inspired by the ancient traditional practice, where patients would stay at the Vaidya's (Doctor), home until fully healed, Ayurvedam Sreenilayam was envisioned and built within Dr. Mali's Nalukettu-traditional home premises. Here, patients are welcomed into a warm, serene, healing environment where they eat, live, and rest in the lap of nature under close observation, and care until recovery. This one-of-a-kind space offers holistic Ayurvedic care rooted in authenticity, compassion, and tradition. Dr. Mali emphasizes personalized healing, using locally sourced medicinal herbs, individualized organic diets, and structured lifestyle guidance. His treatments are designed not just to address illness, but to restore balance, harmony, and vitality in every aspect of life.

Areas of Specialization: Dr. Mali has extensive experience in treating a wide range of conditions, including:

  • Chronic Skin Disorders: Psoriasis, Hidradenitis Suppurativa
  • Mental Health: Depression, Anxiety, Insomnia, Stress
  • Digestive & Autoimmune Disorders: Ulcerative Colitis, Crohn’s Disease, Gastritis
  • Joint & Musculoskeletal Issues: Arthritis, Low Back Pain (Disc Prolapse), Cervical/Lumbar Spondylosis,
  • Respiratory Disorders: Asthma, Allergic Rhinitis
  • Neurological Conditions: Paralysis, Parkinson’s Disease, Early-stage Alzheimer’s
  • Reproductive Health: Infertility, PCOD, Fibroids
  • Lifestyle disease: Diabetes, Obesity
  • Cancer Support: Carcinomas at various stages and locations

Ayurveda, Tantra & Global Teaching:

Beyond his clinical practice, Dr. Mali is a respected teacher of Ayurvedic Tantra and Philosophy, with students from around the world coming to learn under his guidance. He has also conducted Ayurvedic workshops in Lausanne, Geneva, and Moscow, sharing India’s healing philosophy on global platforms.
