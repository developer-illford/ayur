Our Ayurvedic Journey

The Ayur Destination Story

Swagatam!

Since 2004, I have been accompanying my clients on Ayurvedic treatments in Kerala. I spent about ten years working with Ayurvedic doctors on site. This experience not only enriched my practice but also created a bond with my clients during our conversations during their Ayurvedic treatment. Many of them were desperate to fall back into the vicious circle of bad habits, 'obligations' and therefore stress and its consequences after their Ayurvedic treatment. Ultimately, their Ayurvedic treatment got them back in shape, but they felt like they were always back where they started when they got home, without knowing for themselves how to stay physically, mentally, and emotionally fit. It is indeed difficult to move forward alone when the environment seems incompatible and when we do not know the keys to practicing Ayurveda on a daily basis. It is, however, the soul of Ayurveda to engage each of us to put in place the conditions for health, in prevention, on a daily basis. I then began to advise those who wanted it. The AwakeMyEnergy programs were born in their first 'spontaneous' version! Today I am delighted to invite you to participate in the AwakeMyEnergy programs: unique programs, supervised by Ayurvedic doctors from Kerala, designed and constructed with modern educational tools. Intense coaching programs and mentoring stays on the philosophy of Ayurveda and Yoga applied to your daily life to regain shape, energy and move forward in your projects. The objective: to make available to you the lessons and experiences compiled over 25 years to help you experience your inner revolution and thus develop your unsuspected potential.
