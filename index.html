Ayurvedic Cures

If you want to experience an authentic Ayurveda treatment on the breathtaking Malabar coast of Kerala, you've come to the right place. Be it to increase your well-being or to tackle specific health problems such as back pain, stress symptoms or overweight with the help of Ayurveda. Based on our experience, we have put together various treatment packages that are considered selected treatment approaches.

We are all part of a Universal Life Force that permeates every living entity on Earth.This is the underlying conviction behind the Ayurvedic approach. But where it goes beyond being an esoteric concept is in the way it integrates this tenet into its therapeutic and healing methods.

AYURVEDIC STEAM THERAPY

Swedana

The treatment in the image is called Swedana, specifically Bashpa Swedana, where the body is enclosed in a wooden steam chamber and exposed to herbal steam while the head remains outside. This Ayurvedic therapy helps the body release toxins, improves circulation, reduces stiffness, and prepares it for deeper healing as part of Panchakarma.
